About Us

Hello, my name is Daniel and I am a wedding videographer. I film weddings in Austin, Dallas, San Antonio, Houston, New Orleans and all their surrounding areas.

My biggest passion is God, the Creator of the universe. He's the greatest Artist that I admire the most. His love is my inspiration.

I love French literature, comedy, gospel music, vanilla frosty from Wendy's and indian cuisine. Tyler Perry is my favorite movie director. I can watch "Why Did I Get Married?" again and again.

I believe the best wedding film should be both genuinely romantic and authentically fun. Over-posing is not my thing. Your wedding film should reflect you as a couple and your personalities. If you’re the goofy type, then let’s film goofy! Seriously, if your wedding day is eventful, lively and so much fun, your wedding film shouldn’t be boring! That’s why I love capturing the candid, sweet and funny moments, the natural conversations, the unscripted dialogs, the interviews, uncle Joe’s favorite dance move, the unstaged laughter, the authentic tears of joy…all the little details that matter to you and that you would want to relive twenty years from now!

I'll simply be honored to be your wedding videographer and film your wedding with my artistic, documentary and storytelling style. I can't wait to meet with you and know more about the beauty and significance of your special day.

My family means the world to me. I'm married to the most beautiful woman the world has ever known. She's my partner, my best friend, my advisor, my helper, my queen, my confidence and the reason why I'm the happiest man on earth.

God has blessed us with three adorable children. We all love to travel, visit new places, watch NBA games (and argue over who is the real GOAT..), swim, snuggle up in the couch and watch "Facing The Giants". We've watched the series "Everybody Hates Chris" a million times!
